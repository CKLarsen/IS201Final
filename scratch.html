My Favorite Card Games

The "Larsen Family"

Tutorial for "3-2-1 Countdown!"

My name is Chance Larsen. My family likes to play card games. These are some of our favorites, with small descriptions of how the game is played.

  1. Skull King
    • Make bets as to how well your hand will perform.
    • The better you predict your performance, the more points you win!
  2. Cover Your Assets
    • Make pairs of assets in your hand to lay down in front of you.
    • Try to defend your assets from other players.
    • Whoever has the most money at the end (or protected their assets the best) wins!
  3. 3-2-1 Countdown!
    • Discard cards of the same number to lower the sum total of your hand.
    • First one with 5 or less declares "Countdown!" and wins the round!
